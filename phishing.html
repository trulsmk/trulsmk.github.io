Phishing Defense

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers trick users into giving away sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, or financial info. It’s one of the most common threats facing individuals and small businesses in Norway.

How to Recognize Phishing

  • Urgency: Emails that pressure you to act quickly or face consequences.
  • Strange sender addresses: Slight variations of trusted domains (e.g., @paypa1.com instead of @paypal.com).
  • Suspicious links: Hyperlinks that don’t match the displayed URL or download unexpected files.
  • Spelling and grammar: Poor language often signals a scam.
  • Requests for sensitive info: Legitimate companies will never ask for your password via email.

Why This Matters

Based on my research, 15% of SME employees still lack phishing awareness. Training simulations and regular education significantly improve detection rates. Most successful attacks happen because employees don't recognize fake emails.
